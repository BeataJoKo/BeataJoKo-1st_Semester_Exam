About

Who am I? First of all, a professional photographer and a photo technician, specialized in analogue photography and black & white printing, as well as digital preparation and printing. Secondly, a philosopher and a teacher, specialized in logic systems, philosophy of language, and - last but not least - aesthetic. I practiced and made workshops in Danish and Polish schools, including schools for people with a hearing loos and hearing impairment in Poland. Currently I am a student of multimedia design and communication in Copenhagen.

I am left. I am right. I look from both sides now. And marrying science and art.

What I enjoy the most? Observing, Analyzing and Creating.
And what I do to relax? Climbing and Traveling.

Take a look at me again, and you will see a future, shining star of multimedia design.
